PLAN & PLANT

Creating a One Stop Solution which will guide you throughout your journey of parenting a plant. Providing a platform where we can get all the ecological information we need. Giving all the enthusiasts a common ground to communicate as well as support each other. A one-stop web platform where an induvidual can recieve all the help they need regarding plantations customized to their location. The user can customize their search according to their need and demand for the plant including soil specific plants, indoor/outdoor plants, with/without edible resource, etc. The users will also be provided with a discussion platform in order to connect with fellow enthusiasts as well as proffessionals in the field.
